## Interactive interpreter

### Usage

  * Learning Python and testing specific features
  * Interactive calculations
  * Interactive data processing and conversion

### Simple calculations

    >>> 5 + 4
    9
    >>> 2 - 3
    -1
    >>> 10 / 2
    5.0
    >>> 37 // 10
    3
    >>> 37 % 10
    7
    >>> 2 ** 8
    256

### Bonus: Bitwise operations

    >>> bin(0b01010101 & 0b00110011)
    '0b10001'
    >>> bin(0b01010101 | 0b00110011)
    '0b1110111'
    >>> bin(0b01010101 ^ 0b00110011)
    '0b1100110'
